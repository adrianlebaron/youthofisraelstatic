MISSION STATEMENT


WE ARE SONS AND DAUGHTERS OF OUR HEAVENLY FATHER AND WE HAVE TAKEN UPON OURSELVES THE CHALLENGE TO REPRESENT OUR COMMUNITY AND OUR PEOPLE TO PROVIDE SERVICE WHEN CALLED UPON, STAND UP AGAINST OPPOSITION, SET AN EXAMPLE TO OUR PEERS, OUR FAMILY AND CRIME. WE DO THIS BY STANDING FOR ALL THAT IS HONORABLE AND SHARING THE KNOWLEDGE THAT WE HAVE GAINED BY DEED AND EXAMPLE.

We love our community and we enjoy taking care of it
